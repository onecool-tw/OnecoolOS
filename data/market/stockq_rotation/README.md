# StockQ Global Market Rotation Radar

This cache is an opportunity radar consumed by the Monday Fund Intelligence
report. It does not replace the Market Dashboard SSOT, the seven-fund
watchlist, CTA, Alpha, or DCA actions.

Two-stage screen:

1. Rank StockQ's top 15 markets over one, three, and six months. A market
   appearing in all three periods passes; two appearances remain watch-only.
2. For passed markets, read StockQ's related-fund table. A fund must have
   positive one-, three-, six-, and twelve-month performance. Rank survivors
   by 20%/30%/30%/20% within-table percentile weights and show up to three.

Missing tables or values are never estimated. A passed market without a valid
related-fund table is kept as `NO_ELIGIBLE_FUND`.
